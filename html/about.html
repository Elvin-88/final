History of EGC Slimes

EGC slimes were just a concept by Elvin Concepcion during the winter of 2022. Elvin was a college student dealing with stress, anxiety, and numerous sleepless nights. One midnight he found a TikTok video of a young blonde woman named Taylor Swift, and in that video, he saw himself craving for other factors that would alleviate his sad meaningful life. There at that moment, he thought of EGC Slimes. EGC Slimes is now the best-selling slime company in the world. He opened 60 physical stores in the United States. With his first releases called “bye bye blondie”, and “Yaaas Karen”, he surpassed a milestone of 200 million sales in the first year of his company launch in 2022. He truly amazed the world with his products and will continue to do so.

We the people in EGC Slimes intend to provide enjoyment and alleviate the stress of the masses with our products. Slimes which would be for kids, teens, and mature consumers.
